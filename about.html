A B O U T M E

Hello, my name is Lidija.


I'm an aspiring front-end web developer.


Some time ago I decided to change my career path and try something new and exciting.

Because of my interest in technology I wanted to start exploring that field.

After a lot of research, I got to the conclusion that web development was a great way for me to enter the IT world.

Each line of code made me more passionate about coding and creating web pages and apps. Coding is a way for me to express my creativity and satisfy my need for knowledge and constant improvement.

While learning how to code I worked as an online English tutor. I started teaching during my studies at university. I hold a Bachelor's and Master's degree in Environmental Engineering. My love for learning led me to graduate at top of my class.

Since my formal education is not related to web development, I owe my coding skills to vast online resources (freeCodeCamp, W3Schools, Scrimba , YouTube, etc.) and a lot of time, effort, and discipline on my side. Skills that I acquired and still mastering are HTML, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery and ReactJS. I'm sure that with time and experience this list will get longer.
